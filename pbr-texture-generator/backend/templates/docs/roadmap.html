MapMorph Development Roadmap

This page outlines the future development plans for MapMorph, including upcoming features, improvements, and major milestones.

Current Development Stage

MapMorph is currently in its initial release phase, providing essential texture generation features with AI enhancements.

Planned Features & Milestones

Milestone Planned Release Details
v1.1 - 3D visualizer in frontend, AI Tiling Removal, AI Texel Density, Support for Gloss/Spec and Bump workflows Q2 2025 Optimize processing speed for texture generation and improve AI model efficiency.
v1.2 - Material detection overhaul using AI Q3 2025 Support additional texture maps, including height maps and ambient occlusion.
v1.3 - Explanded Custom Presets & Material Libraries Q4 2025 Allow users to create and save custom texture presets for specific workflows (Unreal, Unity, etc.).
v2.0 - Full AI Material Recognition and Segmentation Q1 2026 Advanced AI segmentation to automatically classify materials in an image and generate accurate PBR maps.
v2.5 - Community Marketplace Q2 2026 Introduce a platform where users can share and sell their custom textures and material presets.

Long-Term Vision

  • Integration with 3D modeling software (Blender, Maya, 3ds Max) via plugins and through API access.
  • Real-time texture editing in the browser using a 3D viewer.
  • Cloud-based storage and collaboration tools for developers and artists.
  • Mobile standalone app version for quick texture generation on the go.
