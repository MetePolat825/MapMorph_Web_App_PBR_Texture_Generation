Acknowledgements

MapMorph wouldn't be possible without the inspiration and influence of several outstanding projects in the field of texture generation and 3D asset creation. We want to extend our gratitude to these pioneering tools:

Inspirations

ArmorLab

ArmorLab is an AI-powered texture generation tool that streamlined the process of creating textures for 3D models. MapMorph was heavily inspired by its AI-driven automation approach.

Learn more about ArmorLab

Substance Sampler

Adobe’s Substance Sampler provides advanced material creation workflows that informed MapMorph’s approach to procedural and AI-based texture generation.

Learn more about Substance Sampler

Polycam

Polycam is a 3D scanning application that influenced MapMorph’s approach to photorealistic texture capture and processing.

Learn more about Polycam

Special Thanks

We’d also like to thank the open-source communities that provide the libraries and frameworks used in MapMorph, including:

  • TensorFlow and PyTorch for AI processing
  • Three.js for WebGL rendering
  • OpenCV for image processing
  • Node.js and Flask for backend development

Lastly, a huge thank you to our contributors, testers, and users who help make MapMorph better every day!
