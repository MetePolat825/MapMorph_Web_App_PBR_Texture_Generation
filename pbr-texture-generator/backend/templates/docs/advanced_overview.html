Advanced Features Overview

MapMorph includes a suite of advanced tools that enhance texture creation and optimization. These features provide additional control and automation to help artists, game developers, and designers create high-quality assets with ease.

AI Processing

Leverage AI-powered enhancements to refine and optimize texture maps.

  • AI-Powered Noise Reduction: Removes unwanted artifacts and noise from textures.
  • Smart Edge Detection: Enhances surface details while preserving sharp edges.
  • Auto Balancing: Adjusts brightness, contrast, and color consistency across textures.

AI Segmentation of Materials

Automatically detect and classify materials within an image to generate accurate texture maps.

  • Material Categorization: AI identifies different materials (e.g., wood, metal, stone) in an image.
  • Smart Masking: Generates masks for different material types for easy editing.
  • Custom Material Mapping: Users can manually adjust or override AI-detected materials.

Texel Density Setter

Ensure consistent texel density across all assets for a uniform look.

  • Automatic Texel Density Calculation: MapMorph analyzes textures to determine optimal texel density.
  • Custom Density Input: Manually set a specific texel density value for your textures.
  • Real-Time Visualization: Preview texel density distribution before exporting.

Texture Upscaler

Enhance the resolution of textures while maintaining detail and sharpness.

  • AI-Based Super Resolution: Uses deep learning to upscale textures without losing quality.
  • Supports 2x, 4x, and 8x Scaling: Choose the best resolution for your project.
  • Preserve Fine Details: Maintains the integrity of patterns, textures, and edges.
